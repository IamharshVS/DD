What is Depression?

Depression is a common mental health disorder that causes persistent sadness and loss of interest. It impacts millions worldwide, significantly disrupting daily life.

The Importance of Early Detection:

  • Benefits: Early detection improves treatment, reduces severity, cuts healthcare costs, and enhances awareness.
  • Challenges: Misdiagnosis risks, patient anxiety, high resource demands, and data privacy concerns.
